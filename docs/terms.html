What Pet Passport is

Pet Passport is an iOS utility that summarizes publicly available pet-import requirements and produces a date-sequenced compliance timeline based on the departure date and destination you enter.

What Pet Passport is not

Pet Passport is not legal advice, veterinary advice, a customs broker, or a government service. Pet Passport does not guarantee that following its timeline will result in your pet being admitted to any country. Always confirm final requirements with a USDA-accredited vet and the destination country's customs/agriculture authority before you travel.
